The Glucose-Insulin Simulator is an educational web tool developed for the University of Arkansas Biomedical Engineering Department (Spring 2025). It models blood glucose dynamics using Bergman's Minimal Model with extensions for exercise effects and personalized parameters.

Intended Use: Explore how insulin dosing, exercise intensity, and patient characteristics (age, sex, weight) influence plasma glucose over time.

Not for Clinical Use: This simulator is not a medical device and should not be used for diagnosis, treatment, or clinical decision-making. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for personalized medical advice.
