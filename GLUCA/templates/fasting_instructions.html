Fasting Blood Glucose Instructions

Accurate baseline blood glucose measurements help contextualize your simulation results. Follow these steps for a proper fasting test:

  1. Preparation: Avoid all food and beverages (except water) for 8–12 hours before testing. The best practice is to test first thing in the morning after an overnight fast.
  2. Hygiene: Wash your hands thoroughly with soap and warm water. Dry completely to remove any residual sugar or moisture.
  3. Meter Setup: Insert a new test strip into your glucose meter according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  4. Sample Collection: Use a sterile lancet to prick the side of your fingertip. Wipe away the first drop of blood, then apply the second drop to the test strip.
  5. Reading: Wait for the meter to process (typically 5–10 seconds). Record the value displayed. This reading represents your fasting blood glucose level.

Note: While optional for this simulator, having your fasting glucose can provide useful context. If you choose not to measure, you may proceed directly to the simulator.
